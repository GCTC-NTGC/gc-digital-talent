Welcome to GC Talent

About GC Talent

GC Talent connects people looking for career and development opportunities to Government of Canada equity-based and functional communities. These communities manage talent pools and organize development and mobility opportunities for those with key skill sets, working in various types of roles.

In this ecosystem, users can:

  • Create a reusable profile that showcases their unique set of skills
  • Curate their list of skills in a way that is recognized and valued by Government of Canada managers
  • Learn about equity-based and functional communities within the Public Service that align with their expertise or identity
  • Browse opportunities within the entire platform ecosystem or choose to focus on opportunities offered by specific communities
  • Get found by Government of Canada managers looking to add new talent to their teams

Both portals are still in development. Subscribe to our mailing list to be notified when the portals go live.

Research-Driven Innovation: The Talent Cloud Experiment

Talent Cloud was a 3-year experimental project that explored new approaches to talent recruitment and mobility in the Government of Canada. The project concluded in Spring 2021.

A comprehensive record of the project, including the goals, structure, process, and outcomes, can be found in the Talent Cloud Results Report.
